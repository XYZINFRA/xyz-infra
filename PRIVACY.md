# XYZ INFRA: Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2, 2026

1. **Data Collection:** XYZ INFRA does not collect personally identifiable information (PII) from human users. We collect only technical metadata required for machine settlement, including Wallet Addresses, Agent IDs, and Transaction Hashes.
2. **Usage:** This data is used exclusively to verify payment finality on the Tempo L1 blockchain and to comply with Stripe’s Machine Payments Protocol requirements.
3. **Data Sharing:** We do not sell or trade transaction metadata. Data is only shared with Stripe and Tempo for the purpose of financial settlement and fraud prevention.
4. **Retention:** Transaction logs are maintained as required by global financial regulations for stablecoin settlement.
